Autonomous Cash Matching

AI-powered matching that closes your books

Billtrust's autonomous matching engine ingests every payment format — checks, ACH, wire, lockbox, virtual cards — and resolves them against open invoices in under a second. Trained on billions of historical transactions, the model handles remittance variations, partial payments, and multi-invoice remits that break rules-based systems. Exceptions drop to an assisted workflow where your team resolves only what the AI can't.
